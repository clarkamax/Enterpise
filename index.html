Directional Boring

Underground conduit installation without open trenching — minimal surface disruption for roads, driveways, landscaping, and waterways.

  • Horizontal directional drilling (HDD)
  • Road & driveway crossings
  • Conduit & cable installation
  • Trenchless utility runs
  • Ag & rural site boring
  • Urban & commercial projects

Boring lets us run underground conduit under roads, parking lots, and landscaped areas without tearing up the surface — saving you restoration costs and keeping your site looking clean.

Get a quote →
Hydro Vac

Pressurized water excavation combined with vacuum extraction — the safest way to expose underground utilities and dig in sensitive areas.

  • Safe utility exposure & daylighting
  • Slot trenching for conduit
  • Potholing around live utilities
  • Frozen ground excavation
  • Tight-access & urban sites
  • Post-hole & pier excavation

Hydro vac is the go-to method when digging near live gas, water, or fiber lines. The pressurized water breaks up soil precisely while the vacuum removes it, with zero risk of striking buried utilities.
